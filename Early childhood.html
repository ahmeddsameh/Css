Early Childhood

Although I was born in Alexandria Egypt, I traveled at a young age to Europe and spent most of my childhood growing up in Amsterdam, Netherlands. I grew up on a street called Listerlaan street surrounded by dutch neighbors and friends. Conveniently enough My school was right down the road from where I lived. It was a very friendly and safe community and I was close enough to my friends and neighbors, to a point where they felt like family. Living in the Netherlands and traveling throughout Europe gave me great social skills, most of which I use to this day.

Teen Years

After spending elementary school in the Netherlands, I moved back to egypt. I spent four years there surrounded by relatives and friends. During this time, I also traveled to visit other countries such as the philippines. This constant rhythm of travel, taught me to become an ambivert and to adapt to whatever surroundings I am placed in.

HighSchool

After years in Egypt, my family came to an intrepid decision to move to Canada. I spent most of the year on PEI and in the summer would travel to places such as Toronto, Halifax, Vancouver or Ottawa. That is ofcourse before covid hit.
